Profile

My design principle is to 'Surprise & Delight' the users with unique and intuitive ways to interact with the product I am creating.

Working as the Lead UI + UX Designer for the breakthrough SAAS company Tactiv, I have designed and developed three unique pieces of software for their Grant Management platform to both modernise their interface, and synchronise the software's appearance with the company's rebrand.

In addition, I have freelanced as a Graphic Designer to create signage and assets across digital and print mediums. These assets varied widely, and ranged from wall mounted glass, to 3D laser cut acrylic, and multi panelled convention booths.
