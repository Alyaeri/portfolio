Enquire Branding

Overview

Enquire is a revolutionary Grant Management system launching to global success, working in partnership with governments all over the world to provide a reliable system for the distribution of government funds to community improvement projects all over their country.

Recently they have undergone a rebrand, for which they require new logs for their range of solution streams. Working with the original 'Grantcycle Management' logo, I designed 5 variants for each respective solution stream. These logos were created with versatility in mind, as such, they can be used as stand alone, or an inverted group logo.
