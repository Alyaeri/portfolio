Android Reimagined

The 'Android Reimagined' concept is a self improvement project that looked at the Android OS, and explored a redesign of the interface, while conforming to Google's Material Design Guidelines. It investigates playful interactions between bold images, and semi transparent shapes and colours, to create a cohesive and seamless experience between applications of all types
